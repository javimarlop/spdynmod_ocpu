Spatio-Dynamic Wetland Plant Communities Model

A spatio-dynamic modelling package that focuses on three characteristic wetland plant communities in a semiarid Mediterranean wetland in response to hydrological pressures from the catchment. The package includes the data on watershed hydrological pressure and the initial raster maps of plant communities but also allows for random initial distribution of plant communities. Ongoing developments of the package focus on offering easy to use tools for creating other spatio-dynamic models.
